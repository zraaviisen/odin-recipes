How to make carbonara

Description

This is a delicious recipe on how to make a traditional pasta carbonara.

Quick, easy and rich in flavor.

Ingredients: (4 servings)

  • 500g Spaghetti
  • 200g Bacon
  • 2 Egg yolks
  • Black pepper
  • Tarragon
  • Salt
  • 1 dl Cream (optional)

Steps:

  1. Heat 2l water in a cooking pot on high heat and add a teaspoon of salt.
  2. Put in the pasta once the water is boiling. For al-dente, boil for 6-7 mins. Drain the water but save some for later.
  3. Chop the bacon into 1x1cm pieces and add to a frying pan with a small amount of oil.
  4. Fry the bacon on high heat until its ready. It's done right before it's too crispy.
  5. Separate the yolks of the eggs and put them in a small bowl.
  6. Use some of the pasta water together with the yolks and mix with a fork until creamy.
  7. (For a silkier carbonara, add the cream.)
  8. Pour the mix into the cooking pot with pasta. Add the bacon and stir until it becomes creamy.
  9. Add the seasoning and you're done. (Don't be afraid to use alot of black pepper.)

    10. There you have it, a perfect carbonara!
